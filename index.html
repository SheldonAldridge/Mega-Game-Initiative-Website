About Us

Who are we?

Mega Games Initiative is a games design association, developing and facilitating megagames to entertain, educate and inspire.

What do we do?

We design and facilitate in person megagames for education, development, teambuilding and entertainment.

Our Visions:

Educational

To empower learners to imagine the world more complexly through the medium of megagames.

As an educational tool we are a new organisation that promotes megagames as a tool for authentic, scenario-based experimental learning. learning through play as a method that encourages the development of critical and creative thinking, and megagames are an innovative way to bring these learning experiences to learners. Also they're just plain fun!

Entertainment

To encourage teamwork and play through megagames, offering both entertainment and community to players.
